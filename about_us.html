About Us

Our Story

At Nike, we believe in the power of sport to elevate human potential. Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, our journey began as a distributor for Onitsuka Tiger, now known as ASICS. In 1971, we launched our first product, the Nike Cortez, and soon rebranded ourselves as Nike, named after the Greek goddess of victory.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world. We strive to create products, services, and experiences that help athletes achieve their goals, whether on the field, in the gym, or in everyday life.

Our Values

  • Innovation: We are constantly pushing the boundaries of technology to develop cutting-edge products that enhance performance.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting sustainability through innovative materials and practices.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: We embrace diversity in all its forms, believing that it drives creativity and innovation.
  • Community Engagement: We actively support local communities through various programs that encourage youth participation in sports.

Join Us

At Nike, we are not just a brand; we are a movement. We invite you to join our community of athletes, innovators, and dreamers. Together, let's redefine the boundaries of sport and fitness.
